ABOUT

the game

inspired by...

‍Fish Beat Down (title to be decided by the community) is a play-to-earn web-based game for raining down blow after blow on your opponent’s fish. There are combos, special moves, special weapons, and blood-curdling finishes.

Fishes about to get beat!

Fight against other fish peer-to-peer, or play the AI, who is jacked to the gills!

Your NFT will grant you access to the training dojo, where you can level up and prep your fish for gameplay.

Your fish isn’t like other NFTS — it has special gaming attributes that can enhance its performance. The more hours and wins logged, the more badass your fish becomes.

There’s much more to come! We’ve assembled an elite team of seasoned experts to handle every aspect of this game. studio-produced music, effects and game mechanics built by an experienced game studio, but REFINED BY YOU the community.
